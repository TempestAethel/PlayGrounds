# Gaming Grinds 🎮💪

> “Not all my games are documented, and not everything I document is something I truly love — it’s just what I happened to save along the way.”

---

This is my casual gaming archive.

It’s not a complete record of everything I play, and it’s not a curated list of my favorite games either. It’s somewhere in between — a collection of random moments I decided to keep.

Sometimes I play a game and save nothing. Sometimes I play a game and save a lot. There’s no pattern, and that’s the point.

---

## 📁 Folder Structure

Everything is grouped by platform:

📁 Android -- Mobile games and random captures  
📁 PC -- PC games, highlights, and gameplay moments  
📁 Web -- Browser-based games and related content  

Inside each platform folder:

📁 PDFs -- Guides, notes, walkthroughs, or references I happened to save  
📁 Screenshots -- In-game moments, achievements, or visuals I liked  
📁 Videos -- Gameplay clips and highlights  
📁 Guides -- Mixed collections of game-related content (notes, images, clips, info dumps) when I felt like grouping things together  

---

## 🧠 How It Works

- I don’t document every game I play  
- I don’t only save my favorite games either  
- Some games get full attention, most don’t  
- What gets saved is completely random and based on the moment  
- The structure only exists to keep things from becoming messy  

---

## 🎯 Purpose

This is just a personal gaming memory space:
- not a catalog  
- not a completion log  
- not a “best of” list  

Just fragments of gameplay I happened to keep.
